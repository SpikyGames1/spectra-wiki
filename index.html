What is Spectra?

Spectra is a discord bot made for the full control of a server with quick and easy commands. It has a variety of functions that allow for moderation, server interactivity, and much more. its still in development but we are proud to announce that it is ready for launch. We will update it regularly.

The main use of Spectra was to allow the quickest way for editing, managing and moderating your server, whilst keeping people engaged in activities this bot offers without having to pay a single dime for anything.

Who made Spectra?

Spectra is made by SpikyGames in 2023 , originally just meant to be a Economic bot, but later as the MEE6 drama happened, Spiky decided to turn Spectra into a multi-purpose bot supporting both economy and moderation, claiming to make the server more featureful without having to pay a single cent for a single feature
